Brainhack Austin is coming
May 2 - 4, 2018!

Rooms 1.202, 1.204 and 1.208, Health Discovery Building, 1701 Trinity Street, Austin Texas, 78701

Brainhack Austin 2018 is a regional event that will occur a part of Brainhack Global 2018. The goal of Brainhack is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds from around the world to collaborate on innovative open science projects related to the brain.

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org.

Please join the the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.

For more information:

brainhackorg@gmail.com
